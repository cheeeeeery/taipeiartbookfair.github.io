OPEN CALL

Welcome to Taipei Art Book Fair 2019!

We appreciate your support as we’re heading towards the fourth annual Taipei Art Book Fair. Welcome aboard! But just before you go ahead and submit your application please bear with us for a moment. Please carefully read the following conditions and by applying to participate, you are accepting these conditions and agree to be bound by them.

Fair Date

Preview: 2019/10/18 (Fri) 14:00 - 21:00

Fair Hours: 2019/10/19, 20 (Sat-Sun) 12:00 - 20:00

Venue: Taipei SongShan Cultural and Creative Park, No.133, Guangfu South Road, Xinyi District, Taipei City 11072, Taiwan goo.gl/maps


About the participation

This application form is for participants who wish to have their own table or booth in which to exhibit and sell their publications or artworks. As we receive great numbers of applications, we may decline the application based on the submitted information due to the limitation of the space.


Deadline

Application deadline is July 19th (Friday 24:00). We will notify you regarding your participation by August 2nd (Friday 24:00). Payment must be made by August 16th (Friday 24:00).


At the Fair

The organizing committee will decide the position of booths according to the overall layout of the space. No booth position preference will be accepted before nor after the floor plan has been made. Please make sure that your booth is attended at all times during the fair. No booth position changes will be allowed. Wi-Fi access and power sockets are not guaranteed. We recommend bringing your own mobile hotspot and extension cord.


Booth Types

Curation Booth 580 USD (Including tax)

For participants who wish to work on a bigger scale of space during the book fair. Application will need to include a complete plan of space use. Walls and up to two full-size tables are included. The size of the booth space is around 4.5 square meters.


One Regular Table 110 USD (Including tax)

For participants who wish to use a full-size table. Use of walls is not guaranteed.

Outdoor Type 35 USD (Including tax)

For participants who wish to use a full-size table outdoors with a roof.

* Table size approximately 150x60 cm.

Facts from 2018 Taipei Art Book Fair

  • 180 exhibitors (75 international booths from 14 countries + 105 local booths)
  • 6,000 paid visitors

開幕日: 2019/10/18 (五) 2PM - 9PM

書展期: 2019/10/19, 20 (六-日) 12PM - 8PM

地點: 松山文創園區, 臺北市信義區光復南路133號 goo.gl/maps


申明

該申請表適用於希望擁有自己的桌子或展位以展示和出售其出版物或藝術品的參與者。當我們收到大量申請時，由於篇幅所限，我們可能會根據提交的信息拒絕該申請。


最後期限

申請截止日期為7月19日（星期五24:00），我們將在8月2日（星期五24:00）通知您您的參與。必須在8月16日（星期五24:00）之前付款。


在展會上

組委會將根據展位的整體佈局確定展位的位置。為了公平起見，在地板平面製作之前或之後，均不接受對展位位置偏好的要求。


展位類型

策展攤位580 USD（含稅）

對於希望在書展期間擴大工作空間（最好舉辦小型展覽）的參與者。

一張正桌110美元（含稅）

對於希望使用全尺寸表的參與者。

戶外型35美元（含稅）

對於希望使用全尺寸表的參與者。室外帶屋頂。

* 桌子尺寸約150*60厘米。
